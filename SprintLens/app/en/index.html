Photo finish with 2 phones

Set one device at the start point and the other at the finish point. SprintLens connects the start sound and finish video to support race-day timing in the field.

  • Automatic Start Play the start sound from this device and measure from the gun sound.
  • Photo Finish Judge the finish from high-frame-rate video.
  • Manual Timing Record the finish by tapping when the runner crosses the line.
